## Abstract
This paper focuses on the design and development of an All-Terrain Vehicle (ATV)
chassis for BAJA-type competitions. The study includes material selection,
cross-section optimization, frame design considerations, and structural analysis
using ANSYS to ensure safety, strength, and durability under impact conditions.

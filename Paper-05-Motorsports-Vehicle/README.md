# Design and Manufacturing of Motorsports Vehicle

## Publication
- International Journal of Engineering Research and Applications (IJERA)
- Volume 6, Issue 5
- May 2016

## Abstract
This paper discusses the design and manufacturing of a go-kart for motorsports
competitions. The work includes chassis design, steering, braking, transmission,
and safety innovations. CAD modeling, FEA analysis, and multi-body dynamics
are used to optimize performance and safety.
